BigButton Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 21, 2026

Summary: BigButton does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All your data stays on your device.

Overview

BigButton is a simple habit tracking widget for Android. Your privacy is important to us, and we've designed the app to work entirely offline with no data collection.

Data Storage

All data created by BigButton is stored locally on your device:

  • Widget state (done/not done status)
  • Settings (reset period, reset time)
  • History (completion records for the calendar view)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Data Collection

BigButton does not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Any other data

Permissions

BigButton requests the following permissions:

  • Schedule Exact Alarms - Required to reset your widget at the configured time each day. This permission is used solely for the auto-reset feature and does not access any personal data.
  • Receive Boot Completed - Required to reschedule the reset alarm after your device restarts.

Third-Party Services

BigButton does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics, or advertising frameworks.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time:

  • Use the "Clear History" button in the Calendar tab to delete tracking history
  • Uninstall the app to remove all data completely

Children's Privacy

BigButton does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
